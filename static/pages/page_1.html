Discord

  • 【👋】welcome-center
    Here you can find the people who have joined the server with the date, time and name.

  • 【📢】announcements
    Within this channel, Command Staff will post important information regarding the department.

  • 【📢】press-releases
    This channel is where you can find all of the SAHP Press Releases that are written up. These releases will cover a variety of topics related to the SAHP.

  • 【📁】documentation
    Here you can find all important documents within the department. => E.G SOP, Application, Roster, Report Form.

  • 【💬】department-chat
    This is the main channel for conversing with fellow members and staff.

  • 【🔥】memes
    Post your top quality memes for the department to see, Keep a level of appropriateness.

  • 【📷】media-center
    Any media, clips and pictures you'd like to share with the department post here.

  • 【📥】suggestions
    Use this channel to suggest things to the department staff and voice your opinion.

  • 【📢】shout-outs
    If you work hard enough in the field supervisors will notice! Here you can be shouted out for your hard work.

  • 【❓】ask-a-sgt
    If you have any questions or need any help / support, here you can ask for a seargent to respond

  • 【🆔】role-request
    Here you can update your callsign in the discord, Find your current callsign on the SAHP Roster. If you need to request a name, is where it will be put.

  • 【🆘】general-request
    This is where you can make a ticket with Staff of the department and yourself if you need further assistance to resolve an issue.

  • 【📄】roles
    External Roles Here you can gain the roles of SWAT, or if from another department, the roles of that department.

  • 【📋】Support VC
    If needed, you may go into a Support VC and wait if you are directed to join, you would join the Support Waiting Room.

  • 【📢】training-announcements
    Here upcoming planned trainings will be posted, Please react your attendance when one is posted.

  • 【🚗】ride-along-request
    This channel is where you will request a one of the 3 types of ride alongs. Click the appropriate button in the channel and a ticket will be made with further information.

  • 【📝】fto-application
    The application to apply to become a FTO is found here, As well above results of the application will be posted.

  • 【🪑 】FTO Waiting Room
    Once you request a ride along you will communicate with the FTO who has claimed the ticket inside of these channels, Join the waiting room then you will be dragged.
