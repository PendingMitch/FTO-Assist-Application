Traffic Management

Prop Menu

To open the prop menu navigate to the prop meu go to 8 => Object Menu.

In this menu you can spawn props. Use cones, signs and any realistic prop to stop or divert traffic for the safety of the emergency personal or subjects ahead. Scene Command will determine how many lanes are to stay open or if a complete stop of the flow of traffic on the highway is necessary.

Below you can see props being used in an appropriate manner

Speed Zones

To impact the realism and control the local drivers, Use speed zones to stop all locals in the area you set it in.

Open this menu by navigating to 8 => Speed Zone

Ensure that speed zones and props are removed before leaving the scene
