Onboarding Cadets Guide

Introduction

Note to the cadet the following:

  • That they are welcomed into the SAHP
  • Introduce yourself, rank, and any Junior FTO that may be shadowing this process
  • That this session is educational - we will be looking through the educational training guide that can be found at the Document Portal in the 【📁】documentation channel
  • If they have any questions that they are welcome and encouraged to ask them throughout the process
  • If they need to have a break from the information overload - that is fine - they should ask the FTO training them
  • Ask if they have any outstanding questions from current field activity that they would like to ask before starting the training

    Computer Aided Dispatch

    Logging Into CAD

    (1) Log into the FivePDRP CAD with your discord account.
    (2) Log into SonoranCAD - if not already please make an account
    (3) Type in the community code found in the 『💻』cad-mdt channel along with the approrpiate permission code.
    (4) Enter the `Police` dashboard

    Utilising the CAD - Changing User Information

    (1) Choose the red marker that states `NOT SET` in the top left of the page


    (2) Change all information to appriopriate fields. - NOTE: some spots may be left blank.

    Utilising the CAD - Dashboard


    This is the CAD dashboard, you will be able to set yourself 10-6, 10-7, 10-8, etc. This is also where your panic button is and where your active calls are shown. Don’t forget to press the “Self Dispatch” button if there is no active dispatch online.

    Vehicles

    How do you spawn vehicles?

    Navigate to F10 => LEO Toolbox => Vehicles => San Andreas Highway Patrol.

    If you wish to change the way that the vehicle looks, make sure to go to F1 => Vehicle Related Options => Vehicle Otions.
    Change the livery through the Vehicle Livery.
    Change the extras of the vehicle including ram bars, lightbars, etc through Vehicle Extras

    NOTE: some vehicles may have a `matte` look to the vehicle upon initial spawning. To change this you can change you colour to black.

    NOTE: Cadets can only use white top vehicles. Cadets can only use lightbar vehicles. Check the SOP - Section 4.2 for further information.
